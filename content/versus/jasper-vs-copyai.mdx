## Round 1: Long-Form SEO Blog Post

**Prompt:** "Write a 500-word blog intro on 'best alternatives to Notion AI for engineers'."

- **Jasper:** Followed the brief structure, hit the keyword density target, and produced one usable draft out of five.
- **Copy.ai:** Faster, but outputs were thinner and had to be re-prompted twice for depth.

**Winner:** Jasper.

## Round 2: Ad Copy & Short-Form

**Prompt:** "10 Facebook ad headlines for a $29/mo AI writing tool."

- **Jasper:** Solid, but two of the ten read like LinkedIn posts.
- **Copy.ai:** Nailed the punchy, scroll-stopping voice. All ten were usable.

**Winner:** Copy.ai.

## Round 3: Brand Voice Consistency

After feeding both tools 5 sample articles to learn voice:

- **Jasper Brand Voice:** Genuinely captured tone after 3 samples.
- **Copy.ai:** Required more manual prompt steering for tone.

**Winner:** Jasper.
