## The TL;DR Verdict

**Skip the 2,000 words:** Pick **Notion AI** if you live in Notion and want AI on top of your team docs. Pick **Obsidian Copilot** if you own your data, prefer markdown files, and want to bring your own LLM keys.

## Why You Should Trust This Review

I migrated the **same 200-note knowledge base** into both tools and ran identical retrieval, summarization, and writing tasks for 30 days.

---

## Round 1: Knowledge Retrieval ("Ask my notes")

- **Notion AI:** Q&A across your workspace with surprisingly good citations.
- **Obsidian Copilot:** Vault search via embeddings — accurate, but you set up the embeddings yourself.

**Winner:** Notion AI for ease of use; Obsidian Copilot for control.

## Round 2: Writing Inside Notes

- **Notion AI:** Frictionless inline writing, summaries, and translations.
- **Obsidian Copilot:** Excellent — and you can swap models (GPT-4, Claude, local Llama).

**Winner:** Tie.

## Round 3: Data Ownership

- **Notion AI:** Your notes live on Notion's servers.
- **Obsidian Copilot:** Local markdown files. Total ownership.

**Winner:** Obsidian Copilot.

## Pricing Breakdown

- **Notion AI:** $10/mo per user (add-on)
- **Obsidian Copilot:** Free plugin + you pay your LLM API costs (typically $5–15/mo for personal use)

## The Final Verdict

- **Buy Notion AI if:** Your team is already in Notion and you want zero-setup AI.
- **Buy Obsidian Copilot if:** You're a solo knowledge worker who wants markdown ownership and model choice.
